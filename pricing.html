Pricing FAQ

Can I start with one module?

Yes. You can subscribe to a single intelligence module and upgrade later to a full platform plan.

Which plan should I choose first?

Starter is best for small businesses. Growth is better if you need API protection and higher usage.

Do plans include support?

Yes. Free includes basic support, paid plans include email or priority support, and Enterprise includes dedicated support.

Can pricing change as usage grows?

Yes. Enterprise pricing can be customized based on usage volume, modules, traffic, and security requirements.
