Pricing FAQ

Can I start with one module?

Yes. You can subscribe to one intelligence module and upgrade later to a full platform plan.

Which plan should I choose first?

Starter is best for small teams. Growth is better if you need API protection and higher usage.

Is AI Agent Permissions a module?

No. It is a governance and settings control layer included with platform plans.

Do plans include support?

Yes. Free includes basic support, paid plans include email or priority support, and Enterprise includes dedicated support.
