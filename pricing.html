Pricing FAQ

Do full plans include all modules?

Yes. Every full platform plan includes Email Risk, Device Risk, Bot Detection, API Abuse, and Payment Fraud Intelligence.

What changes between plans?

Daily checks, API keys, team seats, and support level increase by plan.

Is AI Agent Permissions sold separately?

No. It is a platform governance and settings layer, not a separate detection module.

Can I start with one module?

Yes. You can subscribe to an individual module and upgrade later to a full platform plan.
