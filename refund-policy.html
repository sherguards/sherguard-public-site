Billing

Refund Policy

SherGuard aims to provide transparent billing, fair customer support, and responsible subscription management for trust intelligence and cybersecurity services.

Subscription Services

Subscription plans provide access to selected SherGuard modules, dashboard functionality, API access, organization features, security monitoring, and trust intelligence systems according to the active plan.

Billing Cycle

Paid subscriptions are billed according to the selected billing cycle. Customers are responsible for managing plan upgrades, downgrades, and cancellations before renewal dates.

Refund Requests

Customers may contact SherGuard support regarding billing disputes, accidental charges, or subscription concerns within a reasonable period after the transaction date.

Abuse & Fraud

Accounts associated with fraud, malicious activity, abuse operations, payment fraud, Terms violations, or unauthorized platform usage may be suspended without refund eligibility.

Cancellation

Customers may cancel subscriptions before the next billing cycle to avoid future charges. Cancellation does not automatically generate refunds for prior billing periods.

Enterprise Agreements

Enterprise plans, custom contracts, onboarding services, and negotiated agreements may follow separate billing and refund arrangements.

Contact

Billing and refund inquiries may be submitted through the contact page or by emailing support@sherguard.com.
